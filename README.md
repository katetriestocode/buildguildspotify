This is the spotify (ew f daniel ek) display i made for buildguild... and then worked on more :P
instead of spotify, it is a pomodoro style study buddy tamagotchi!
It features a tft 1.8" 160x128 screen, a lolin mini esp32, and 3 buttons.
The tamagotchi works so that it encourages study sessions of 30 mins with 5 mins break, switching between different faces such as idle, locked in and tired (which he becomes after studying more than half an hour without breaks, and regenerates energy after a 5 min break) :)
It also displays current study time, current break time left, and total study time.
